The Heart of Sports

The Colosseum is truly a wonder of the modern world.. It has hosted over 1,000 events in more than 20 different sports during its 20 year history. Much like its ancient namesake, this arena is massive. It seats nearly 100,000 people, allowing for high profile events such as professional baseball, basketball and football games. For sports fanatics everywhere, the Colosseum is a must-see.

About the Colosseum

One of the only stadiums of its kind, the Colosseum has been the site of events such as basketball games, gymnastic competitions, hockey games, and tennis matches. The arena can be converted to accomodate virtually any athletic competition. It boasts an olympic-sized swimming pool, which can be closed to allow for a basketball court, ice rink, track, or other playing field to be positioned on top. In addition to enjoying the action down on the field, visitors to the Colosseum will also be treated to some of the finest dining in any venue. The various restaurants located around the concourse of the dome provide delicious options such as steak, lobster, and caviar, while the concession stands offer more conventional food such as hotdogs, pizza and beer.
